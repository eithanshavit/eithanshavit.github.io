The inspiration for this unique desk lamp comes from a stone candle holder my sister got at the Japanese Tea Garden in San Francisco.

I had the design planned in my head, but making an accurate sketch was difficult to do by hand. I created a complete 3D model of the lamp using Autodesk Inventor. That helped me tweak the design to look just right. I then used Illustrator to layout the 2D parts to cut, and carved it on the ShopBot.

A standard light bulb is held by a porcelain socket, and wired using a colorful cord I got at Color Code Company.
