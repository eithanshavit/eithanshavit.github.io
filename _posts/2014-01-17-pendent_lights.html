Pendent lights are so expensive! So I made these slick wooden and brass lights.

priority: 100 ---

We recently moved into a new place, and we were looking for pendent lights for our bathroom. After a long search we could not find anything that suited our budget and needs.

I made these lights with some parts I found on the web, and a wooden canopy I designed and carved on the ShopBot.
